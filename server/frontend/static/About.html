About AutoReview

Your trusted partner in automotive dealership reviews and consumer advocacy

Our Story

Founded in 2015, AutoReview began as a small team of automotive enthusiasts frustrated by the lack of transparent information about dealership experiences. What started as a passion project has grown into the most comprehensive dealership review platform in North America.

2015

AutoReview founded with first 50 dealership reviews

2018

Reached 10,000 verified customer reviews

2020

Launched our Dealer Certification Program

2023

Expanded coverage to all 50 states

Our Mission

To empower car buyers with transparent, unbiased information about dealership experiences while helping reputable dealers showcase their commitment to customer satisfaction.

What We Do

  • Collect and verify customer reviews of dealership experiences
  • Conduct secret shopper evaluations
  • Rate dealerships on pricing transparency, customer service, and facility quality
  • Recognize top-performing dealers with our Certified Excellence awards
  • Provide educational resources for car buyers

Our Values

  • Integrity: We never accept payment for positive reviews
  • Accuracy: Every review is verified before publication
  • Transparency: Our rating criteria are publicly available
  • Fairness: Dealers can respond to all reviews
  • Innovation: Constantly improving our evaluation methods
  • Advocacy: We stand with consumers
